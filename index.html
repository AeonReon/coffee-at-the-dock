Our Story

From a Wee Caravan to Coleraine's Favourite Cafe

When Covid hit in 2020, Frances Galbraith did what any good Northern Irish woman would do — she grabbed a caravan, parked it on an abandoned plot by the River Bann, and started making coffee for the community.

What started as a way to bring people together during lockdown grew into something special. A burger van appeared. Then a pizza hut. Then picnic tables. Before long, Coffee at the Dock was the place to be — dog walkers, families, friends — everyone gathering by the river with a flat white and a traybake.

In late 2024, we had to move from our original spot. But the Coleraine community rallied behind us (over 400 people spoke up!), and now we're back — just five minutes down the road at Castle Lane, Waterside. Same great coffee. Same warm welcome. New views.

What We Serve

More Than Just a Coffee Shop

From proper barista coffee to hearty hot food — we do it all fresh, and we do it right.

Coffee & Tea

Flat whites, lattes, cappuccinos, americanos — plus a proper cup of tea. All made with care, not rushed out the door.

Burgers, Wraps & Hot Food

Our famous Pepper Burger, BBQ chicken pizza, chicken wraps, spicy chips, and a proper Irish stew when the weather calls for it.

Bakes & Traybakes

Homemade scones, chocolate brownies, tea cake, and whatever else Frances has been baking this morning. Get in early — they go fast.

Why Locals Love Us

This Isn't Your Average Cafe

Riverside Setting

Enjoy your coffee with views of the River Bann. Spot herons, cormorants, and if you're lucky, the odd kingfisher. There's nothing else like it in Coleraine.

Bring Your Dog

Dogs aren't just welcome — they're celebrated. We've got water bowls, treats, and plenty of other four-legged friends for yours to meet. This is dog walker HQ.

Community First
